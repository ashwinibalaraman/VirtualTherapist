A test to measure thought coherence

Word associations is a simple exercise to measure Thought Coherence in people.

"Loose word associations" is the fundamental concept underlying formal Thought Disorders seen in psychiatric patients.

We can expect word association to be less usual in patients with Thought Disorder.



This test is an automation of Elvevåg, B., Foltz, P. W., Weinberger, D. R., and Goldberg, T. E. (2007). Quantifying incoherence in speech: An automated methodology and novel application to schizophrenia. Schizophrenia Research EXPERIMENT 1: SINGLE WORD ASSOCIATIONS
